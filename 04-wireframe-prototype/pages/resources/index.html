Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about packaging and services

Product & Pricing Questions

What are the standard packaging sizes available?

We offer various sizes including 6, 12, 18, 24, and 30-piece egg packaging, plus customized options for bakery and fruit products.

How is minimum order quantity (MOQ) determined?

MOQ varies by product type and customization requirements. Standard products start from 500 pieces, while custom orders have different minimums.

What factors affect packaging pricing?

Key factors include material type, size, quantity, customization level, printing requirements, and shipping destination.

Are bulk discounts available?

Yes, we offer tiered pricing with volume discounts. Contact our sales team for detailed pricing based on your specific requirements.

Service & Support Questions

How do I request free samples?

Simply contact our sales team with your product requirements, and we'll arrange sample delivery within 3-5 business days.

What is the custom design process?

Our process includes requirement analysis, design proposal, sample production, customer approval, and mass production.

How long does production typically take?

Standard orders: 7-10 days. Custom orders: 10-15 days. Large volume orders may require additional lead time.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept wire transfers, credit cards, PayPal, and L/C for international orders. Payment terms are negotiable for long-term partners.
