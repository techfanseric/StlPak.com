Material Comparison: PET vs PP

The choice between PET and PP materials depends on your specific requirements for transparency, durability, cost, and environmental considerations.

PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate)

Advantages:

  • Crystal clear transparency for product visibility
  • Excellent barrier properties for freshness
  • Recyclable (PET #1)
  • High strength and durability
  • Food grade certified

Best Applications:

  • Retail packaging requiring product visibility
  • Premium egg products
  • Branded packaging

PP (Polypropylene)

Advantages:

  • Cost effective for large volumes
  • Available in various colors
  • Good chemical resistance
  • Lightweight design
  • Recyclable (PP #5)

Best Applications:

  • Wholesale and bulk packaging
  • Cost-sensitive markets
  • Colored branding requirements
