About StlPak

Global food packaging manufacturer with 8 years of excellence, serving renowned brands worldwide

Company Introduction

Shijiazhuang Shituli Packaging Materials Co., Ltd. (StlPak) was established in 2017 as a professional food packaging manufacturer. With 8 years of development, we have become an influential packaging solution provider in the industry.

Our factory is located in Yangzhou Economic Development Zone, Jiangsu Province, covering approximately 30,000 square meters with 6 advanced production lines and an annual capacity of 1 billion packaging units. Our office is in Qingpu District, Shanghai, providing convenient access for premium customer service.

We consistently adhere to the business philosophy of quality as our lifeline, innovation as our driving force, and customer-centric approach, providing premium packaging products and professional services to our clients.
